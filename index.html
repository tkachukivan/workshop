check tire tread depth

Driving with insufficient tread depth might prevent your tires from making proper contact with the road. Are your tires in good shape? See this chart to find out.

Checking your tires’ tread depth is easier than you think. view how-to

tread depth action
6/32˝ (4.8mm) Your tires have sufficient tread
5/32˝ (4mm) Consider replacing your tires if wet/snowy road conditions are a concern
4/32˝ to 3/32˝ (3.2mm to 2.4mm) You should consider a tire replacement or closely monitor your tires
2/32˝ (1.6mm) Your tires are considered legally bald and need to be replaced
